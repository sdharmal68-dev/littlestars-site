About Us

At Little Stars Development Centre, we believe every child is unique and deserves a nurturing space to grow, learn, and shine.

Our team is dedicated to supporting children with developmental delays, behavioral challenges, and learning difficulties through structured therapy, compassion, and collaboration with families.

Let your little star shine bright with us! ⭐
